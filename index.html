SUBMIT

Fully automated futuristic & intelligent luxury 1, 2 and 3 BHK apartments with a multitude of amenities located amidst green cover at Sompura Gate, Sarjapur Road

For the generation of today,
tomorrow & future.

Soul Tree is made for the free-thinking generation of today. A generation defined by their ideas rather than individualities. For whom home is more than just space and open spaces serve as their platform to meet, greet, mingle, enjoy, grow & so much more. Our tribute to the generation of today, tomorrow & future.

Meet, Greet & Talk

From lobbies of the living areas, various seating clusters in & around the project to the corridor spaces, there are various thoughtfully designed ‘pause points’ to incorporate enthusiastic welcomes and lingering goodbyes. Wholesome architectural enhancements for you to meet, greet & talk.

Mingle, Mix & Laugh

Soul Tree is sprinkled with an array of open spaces including dedicated senior citizen seating areas meant to merge the social and the personal with group-friendly and quiet spaces. The green patch between the living areas is created with landscapes – lush greenery interspersed with seating facilities and calming water bodies where you can mingle, mix & laugh.

Play & Enjoy

With multiple kids-play areas scattered around the project & specifically designed toddlers play areas accompanied by parents seating benches, we aim to cater to the demands of each & every age group. There is a Basket Ball Court, motorised Cricket Nets, Skating & Biking Ring and so much more for you to play & enjoy to the fullest.

Relax & Rejuvenate

It’s a space that is designed to serve today’s global lifestyles and new-age living. Reflexology Barefoot Paths with Pebbles are meant to give you a relaxing break from your hectic lifestyle while the elevated east-facing sunrise court, Yoga zone, various green parks including a butterfly garden, walking path, multiple outdoor gyms & around the project jogging path will help you to relax & rejuvenate.

Unite & Celebrate

Soul Tree lends its name from a rare auspicious banyan tree centring the Amphi Theatre, a uniquely designed space to encourage the celebrations of every nature. Adding to an already impressive Clubhouse packed with world-class facilities & the project dedicated to giving you a cause to unite & celebrate the life every day.

Feel the light

Our rooms, terraces & balconies are designed to take advantage of the Bangalore sun and bathe in natural light. The homes are compact and smart with good views, light and fresh air. All you need for a productive day and a good night’s sleep.

Feel the design

A work of art inside and out. At Modern Spaaces, the passion for design transcends architecture and is reflected in the utmost care in the selection of equipment and materials. Form, function and robustness, in perfect harmony.

Feel good

At Soul Tree, quality of life is the absolute priority. Our clubhouse attests to that. The 4000 sq ft clubhouse has a half indoor, partially covered swimming pool for adults and toddlers. It is designed to keep all your needs in mind from a host of indoor games, Badminton & Squash Court to a fully-equipped gym, a Library, Lego Room, Market Retail, ATM, Pharmacy & Laundry.

Feel Smart

Our homes come with fully automated home automation facility. Now control your entire home with just a mobile app. Unlock the future in our smart homes and experience customized, luxury living.

Feel green

We believe in fair & efficient sustainable practices. Being aware of the environment we live in is a decision we’ve made in the design and functionality of the space. We have taken measures to save water, energy, and hopefully the planet, in the long run.

    A perfect location for a perfect home

    Sompura Gate, Sarjapur Road, located in south-east Bangalore, is one of the fastest-growing parts of the city which makes it perfect & favoured property investment destinations. Despite it's close proximity to the IT hubs of the city it still retains the peaceful & serene aspect missing from almost every other part of the city which certainly are heading towards saturation.


    The real estate demand in this location has seen a ten folds increase. Sarjapur Road enjoys robust residential demand and excellent connectivity to the IT hubs & hotspots like Koramangala, Whitefield, HSR, Electronic City, Marathahalli, Silk Board etc. The planned Metro rail extension, Outer Ring Road and the proposed eight-lane Peripheral Ring Road [PRR] which will reduce the travel time to airport considerably are expected to boost the realty sector here.
